Why this exists

I'm Grant. I'm not a financial advisor, a lender, or anyone with letters after my name — I'm someone who got handed a mortgage pre-approval worksheet, a compound interest formula, and a debt payoff spreadsheet at different points in my life and felt like I needed a translator each time.

So I built FinanceScored: three calculators that do exactly what they say, show you the actual numbers behind the result, and don't make you create an account or hand over your email just to see them.

How it works

Every calculator on this site uses standard financial formulas — the same ones lenders and financial planners use as a starting point. Type in your numbers, get a real answer in seconds, no signup required.

If you want a copy of your results emailed to you, that's optional — we only use that email to send the one report you asked for.

What this isn't

FinanceScored gives you a starting point, not personalized financial advice. Every result is an estimate based on the numbers you enter. Always confirm specifics with a licensed financial professional before making a major decision — a mortgage, a major investment, or a debt payoff plan that affects your credit.
